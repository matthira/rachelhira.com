About

Artist statement, biography, exhibitions, commissions, and studio information.

Rachel Hira creates refined, expressive works with a focus on atmosphere, texture, and restraint.

Her work explores softness, structure, memory, and negative space. Each piece is composed with a quiet visual language, allowing color and surface to carry emotional weight.

For commissions, availability, or studio inquiries, contact hello@rachelhira.com.
